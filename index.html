PC gaming vs Console gaming

Skilled gaming

Skilled gaming is gaming that involves you to have skill in many games that you actually need to have skill in like Fortnite, Call of duty, League of Legends, Fifa, and Halo. These games are games that you have to practice getting better at and have tournaments. For example Fortnite is having their World cup in New York City on July 26-28 and hundreds of skilled pros are practicing and playing the qualifiers and qualifying every week to go to New York City to win money. The tournaments for Call of Duty are different, they have 4 of the best Call of Duty players on a team for 2 teams and they have to do a 4v4 and whichever team wins gets the money and the teams that compete are FaZe, Optic, 100T, and Luminosity but recently there hasn't been that many because of the Fortnite tournament and also because not that many people play Call of Duty anymore.
